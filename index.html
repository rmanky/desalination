A Policy Study considering Desalination in the United Arab Emirates

The Problem

The Sharjah Electricity and Water Authority (or SEWA) lack sufficient guidelines and policies on the topic of desalination, negatively impacting the operational and economical efficiency of their desalination plants. Brine that results from desalination can also negatively impact the surrounding marine environment, and proper regulations are required to limit the impact.

The Process

Students from Worcester Polytechnic Insitute in Massachusetts and the American University of Sharjah are collaborating on pulling relevant information from policies of various regions, guided by the input of experts from around the world. We will also hold focus groups with students from AUS to gain further insight on the impact of such policies on the future generation(s) of Sharjah.

The Product

As a final submission from our project, we will present SEWA with a policy brief on recommendations for the creation and implementation of a comprehensive desalination policy. The proposal will give SEWA insight into creating a desalination policy that is both beneficial and sustainable to the residents and their environment.
